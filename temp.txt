Generate Sample Data
Generate a comprehensive mock fine-tuning dataset for Alpenglow Assist as JSONL-style conversation examples. Include all fields needed for the full workflow: unique conversation_id, topic, intent, user_message, assistant_response, escalation_flag, difficulty, tone, and source_notes. Cover these categories evenly: onboarding, troubleshooting, account access, billing basics, workflow/how-to questions, and safe escalation cases. Include a mix of short and medium-length support conversations, realistic SaaS customer phrasing, and a few intentional anomalies for cleaning such as duplicates, near-duplicates, contradictory answers, off-brand tone, incomplete responses, and mislabeled topics. Ensure the dataset is large enough to support deduplication, balancing, and validation splitting in later steps. Keep all responses concise, helpful, and customer-facing, with no policy-violating or overly technical content.

Define Support Scope
Build the bot around Haven’s real support needs by identifying the exact customer questions it must handle, the cases it must refuse, and the situations that require escalation. Focus on Haven’s home security products, monitoring plans, billing, account access, device setup, and troubleshooting workflows so the bot is tightly aligned to the company’s support operations and customer journey.

Tips
Treat scope as a policy artifact, not just a feature list. The strongest support bots are explicit about what they will answer, what they will not answer, and when they must hand off—especially for account, billing, and safety-related requests.

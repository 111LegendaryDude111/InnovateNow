# Launch announcement post

**Prompt Name:** Launch announcement post  
**Category:** Social media posts  
**Persona/Role:** Senior social media strategist  
**Goal:** Create a concise launch post that explains what is new and why the audience should care.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are announcing [PRODUCT NAME] or [FEATURE NAME] for [AUDIENCE]. The launch solves [CUSTOMER PROBLEM] and the main proof point is [SOURCE FACT OR APPROVED CLAIM].
Objective: Write 3 social media post options for [CHANNEL].
Style: Clear, specific and benefit-led.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE], with awareness level [AWARENESS LEVEL].
Response: Return a table with columns: Hook, Post Copy, CTA, Hashtags. Keep each post under [WORD LIMIT] words. Do not invent metrics, customer names or unsupported claims.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[FEATURE NAME]`, `[AUDIENCE]`, `[CUSTOMER PROBLEM]`, `[SOURCE FACT OR APPROVED CLAIM]`, `[CHANNEL]`, `[TONE]`, `[AWARENESS LEVEL]`, `[WORD LIMIT]`  
**Example Output:**

| Hook | Post Copy | CTA | Hashtags |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reports should not take all Friday. | Meet Atlas Analytics: a faster way for lean finance teams to turn raw subscription data into board-ready revenue reports. Built for teams that need clarity before the next close. | See the launch notes | #FinanceOps #Analytics #SaaS |

**Best Practices/Tips:** Use a single customer problem per post. Replace `[SOURCE FACT OR APPROVED CLAIM]` with verified language before drafting. Match `[CHANNEL]` to the expected length and style.

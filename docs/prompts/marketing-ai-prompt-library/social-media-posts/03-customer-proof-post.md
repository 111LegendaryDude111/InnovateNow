# Customer proof post

**Prompt Name:** Customer proof post  
**Category:** Social media posts  
**Persona/Role:** Customer marketing writer  
**Goal:** Create a social post from an approved customer proof point.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We have approved customer proof from [CUSTOMER TYPE OR SEGMENT]. The usable proof point is: [APPROVED CUSTOMER PROOF]. The promoted product is [PRODUCT NAME].
Objective: Write 4 social post variants for [CHANNEL] that highlight the proof point without exaggeration.
Style: Credible, specific and plainspoken.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return numbered variants. Include one sentence of post copy and one CTA for each. Do not create new quotes, metrics or customer identifiers.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[CUSTOMER TYPE OR SEGMENT]`, `[APPROVED CUSTOMER PROOF]`, `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[CHANNEL]`, `[TONE]`, `[AUDIENCE]`  
**Example Output:**

1. Mid-market finance teams are using Atlas Analytics to cut manual revenue reporting work before close week begins. CTA: Read the customer story.
2. When reporting depends on spreadsheets, every close creates avoidable risk. Atlas Analytics helped one SaaS finance team move recurring revenue views into a shared workflow. CTA: See how it worked.

**Best Practices/Tips:** Use only approved proof. If the proof is anonymous, keep the segment broad. Avoid turning a single customer result into a universal promise.

# Comparison blog outline

**Prompt Name:** Comparison blog outline  
**Category:** Blog outlines  
**Persona/Role:** Product marketing content lead  
**Goal:** Outline a fair comparison article between approaches, tools or categories.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We need a comparison article for [AUDIENCE] evaluating [OPTION A] versus [OPTION B]. Our product, [PRODUCT NAME], is relevant because [PRODUCT RELEVANCE].
Objective: Create a balanced outline that helps readers choose the right approach.
Style: Objective, practical and buyer-aware.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return Buyer Question, Decision Criteria, Comparison Table Outline, Section Outline, When to Choose Each Option, Product Fit and CTA. Do not attack competitors or invent feature claims.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[AUDIENCE]`, `[OPTION A]`, `[OPTION B]`, `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[PRODUCT RELEVANCE]`, `[TONE]`  
**Example Output:**

**Buyer Question:** Should a SaaS finance team manage revenue reporting in spreadsheets or move to a dedicated analytics workflow?  
**Decision Criteria:** Data freshness, auditability, owner visibility, close speed, implementation effort.  
**When to Choose Spreadsheets:** Early-stage teams with simple data and one owner.  
**When to Choose Atlas Analytics:** Teams with recurring revenue complexity, multiple stakeholders and repeated close delays.

**Best Practices/Tips:** Be fair to both options. Use criteria that buyers actually use. Route competitor-specific claims through legal or product marketing review.

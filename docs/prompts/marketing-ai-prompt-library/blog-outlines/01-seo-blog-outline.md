# SEO blog outline

**Prompt Name:** SEO blog outline  
**Category:** Blog outlines  
**Persona/Role:** SEO content strategist  
**Goal:** Create a search-focused blog outline with clear reader intent and product relevance.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are planning a blog post for [AUDIENCE] searching for [PRIMARY KEYWORD]. Secondary keywords are [SECONDARY KEYWORDS]. The product is [PRODUCT NAME], which helps with [PRODUCT VALUE].
Objective: Create a detailed blog outline that satisfies search intent and naturally supports the product narrative.
Style: Helpful, structured and non-fluffy.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return Title, Meta Description, Search Intent, H1, H2/H3 outline, Product Tie-In, Internal Link Ideas and CTA. Do not invent research citations.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[AUDIENCE]`, `[PRIMARY KEYWORD]`, `[SECONDARY KEYWORDS]`, `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[PRODUCT VALUE]`, `[TONE]`  
**Example Output:**

**Title:** Revenue Reporting Automation: A Practical Guide for SaaS Finance Teams  
**Meta Description:** Learn how SaaS finance teams can reduce manual reporting work, improve close readiness and choose the right automation workflow.  
**Search Intent:** The reader wants to understand options and implementation steps.  
**H2 Outline:** What revenue reporting automation means; common reporting bottlenecks; data quality checks before automation; choosing workflow owners; where Atlas Analytics fits.  
**CTA:** Compare manual and automated reporting workflows.

**Best Practices/Tips:** Define the search intent before writing sections. Keep the product tie-in late unless the keyword is product-aware. Validate keywords with the current SEO tool before publishing.

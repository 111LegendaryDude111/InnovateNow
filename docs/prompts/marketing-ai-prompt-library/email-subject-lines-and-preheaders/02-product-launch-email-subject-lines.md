# Product launch email subject lines

**Prompt Name:** Product launch email subject lines  
**Category:** Email subject lines and preheaders  
**Persona/Role:** Product marketing copywriter  
**Goal:** Create launch subject lines that drive opens without overpromising.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are launching [PRODUCT NAME] or [FEATURE NAME] to [AUDIENCE]. The primary benefit is [PRIMARY BENEFIT]. The proof point or source fact is [SOURCE FACT OR APPROVED CLAIM].
Objective: Write 10 subject line and preheader pairs for a launch email.
Style: Benefit-led and concise.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return a table with Subject Line, Preheader, Benefit Emphasized and CTA Fit. Keep subject lines under [SUBJECT LIMIT] characters. Do not invent dates, discounts or feature availability.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[FEATURE NAME]`, `[AUDIENCE]`, `[PRIMARY BENEFIT]`, `[SOURCE FACT OR APPROVED CLAIM]`, `[TONE]`, `[SUBJECT LIMIT]`  
**Example Output:**

| Subject Line | Preheader | Benefit Emphasized | CTA Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meet Atlas Forecasts | Plan renewals with cleaner account-level revenue signals. | Better planning | See what is new |
| New: Forecast views for finance teams | Turn recurring revenue movement into a clearer planning workflow. | Workflow clarity | Explore the feature |

**Best Practices/Tips:** Confirm launch naming before use. Avoid time-sensitive language unless the send date is final. Keep the benefit close to the subject line.

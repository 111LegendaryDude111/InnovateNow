# Newsletter subject line variants

**Prompt Name:** Newsletter subject line variants  
**Category:** Email subject lines and preheaders  
**Persona/Role:** Lifecycle marketing copywriter  
**Goal:** Generate subject lines and preheaders for a newsletter.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are sending a newsletter to [AUDIENCE]. The main story is [MAIN STORY]. Secondary items are [SECONDARY ITEMS]. The brand voice is [BRAND VOICE].
Objective: Write 12 subject line and preheader pairs.
Style: Clear, specific and inbox-friendly.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return a table with Subject Line, Preheader, Angle and Risk Note. Keep subject lines under [SUBJECT LIMIT] characters and preheaders under [PREHEADER LIMIT] characters. Avoid spammy punctuation and unsupported urgency.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[AUDIENCE]`, `[MAIN STORY]`, `[SECONDARY ITEMS]`, `[BRAND VOICE]`, `[TONE]`, `[SUBJECT LIMIT]`, `[PREHEADER LIMIT]`  
**Example Output:**

| Subject Line | Preheader | Angle | Risk Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Your close-week reporting checklist | Five steps to make revenue reporting easier before month end. | Practical guide | Low |
| A better way to spot revenue risk | See how early signals can help teams act before renewal. | Education | Avoid implying guaranteed churn prevention |

**Best Practices/Tips:** Ask for more options than needed, then shortlist manually. Test clarity before cleverness. Keep the preheader additive instead of repeating the subject line.

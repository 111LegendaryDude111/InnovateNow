# Event follow-up subject lines

**Prompt Name:** Event follow-up subject lines  
**Category:** Email subject lines and preheaders  
**Persona/Role:** Field marketing email strategist  
**Goal:** Create post-event subject lines and preheaders that move attendees toward the next action.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are following up with [AUDIENCE] after [EVENT NAME]. They attended or registered for [SESSION OR TOPIC]. The next best action is [NEXT BEST ACTION]. The brand voice is [BRAND VOICE].
Objective: Write 10 subject line and preheader pairs for a post-event follow-up email.
Style: Timely, useful and specific.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return a table with Subject Line, Preheader, Follow-Up Angle and CTA Fit. Keep subject lines under [SUBJECT LIMIT] characters and preheaders under [PREHEADER LIMIT] characters. Do not imply attendance if the segment only registered.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[AUDIENCE]`, `[EVENT NAME]`, `[SESSION OR TOPIC]`, `[NEXT BEST ACTION]`, `[BRAND VOICE]`, `[TONE]`, `[SUBJECT LIMIT]`, `[PREHEADER LIMIT]`  
**Example Output:**

| Subject Line | Preheader | Follow-Up Angle | CTA Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Your revenue reporting session recap | Revisit the close-week checklist and next steps from the webinar. | Recap | Watch recording |
| Keep the close-week checklist handy | Use the worksheet shared during the SaaS finance session. | Practical resource | Download worksheet |

**Best Practices/Tips:** Split attendees and no-shows before using this prompt. Mention only sessions the segment was invited to or attended. Use the preheader to add value beyond the subject line.

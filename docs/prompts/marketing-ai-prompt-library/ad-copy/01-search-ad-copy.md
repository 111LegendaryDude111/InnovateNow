# Search ad copy

**Prompt Name:** Search ad copy  
**Category:** Ad copy  
**Persona/Role:** Performance marketing copywriter  
**Goal:** Generate search ad headlines and descriptions for a specific keyword group.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are running search ads for [PRODUCT NAME]. The keyword group is [KEYWORD GROUP]. The audience is [AUDIENCE]. Approved claims are [APPROVED CLAIMS].
Objective: Write search ad copy variants.
Style: Direct, high-intent and specific.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return 12 headlines under [HEADLINE LIMIT] characters and 6 descriptions under [DESCRIPTION LIMIT] characters. Group them by angle. Do not invent pricing, guarantees or competitor claims.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[KEYWORD GROUP]`, `[AUDIENCE]`, `[APPROVED CLAIMS]`, `[TONE]`, `[HEADLINE LIMIT]`, `[DESCRIPTION LIMIT]`  
**Example Output:**

**Angle: Close-week reporting**  
Headlines: Revenue Reports Ready Faster; SaaS Revenue Reporting; Reporting Built For Finance  
Descriptions: Give finance teams cleaner recurring revenue views before close. Explore Atlas Analytics.

**Best Practices/Tips:** Validate character limits for the ad platform. Keep one intent per keyword group. Pair each headline with a landing page that supports the claim.

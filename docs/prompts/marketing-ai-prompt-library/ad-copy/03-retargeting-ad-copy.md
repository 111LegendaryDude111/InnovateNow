# Retargeting ad copy

**Prompt Name:** Retargeting ad copy  
**Category:** Ad copy  
**Persona/Role:** Demand generation copywriter  
**Goal:** Create retargeting copy that responds to prior interest with a useful next step.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are retargeting [AUDIENCE] who previously engaged with [PRIOR TOUCHPOINT]. The product is [PRODUCT NAME]. The next best action is [NEXT BEST ACTION].
Objective: Write 6 retargeting ad variants.
Style: Relevant, concise and helpful.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: Return Variant, Primary Text, Headline, CTA and Personalization Boundary. Avoid language that feels invasive or reveals tracking details.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[AUDIENCE]`, `[PRIOR TOUCHPOINT]`, `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[NEXT BEST ACTION]`, `[TONE]`  
**Example Output:**

| Variant | Primary Text | Headline | CTA | Personalization Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Still comparing ways to clean up revenue reporting? Start with a practical checklist built for SaaS finance teams. | Build your reporting checklist | Download now | Mentions topic interest, not individual behavior |
| 2 | See how Atlas Analytics helps finance teams turn recurring revenue movement into a shared close-ready workflow. | Review revenue movement sooner | Watch demo | Uses product value, not tracking detail |

**Best Practices/Tips:** Keep personalization broad. Match the CTA to the prior touchpoint. Exclude customers or leads who should not receive retargeting based on consent or lifecycle rules.

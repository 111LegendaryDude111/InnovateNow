# Paid social ad copy

**Prompt Name:** Paid social ad copy  
**Category:** Ad copy  
**Persona/Role:** Paid social strategist  
**Goal:** Create ad variants for awareness, consideration or conversion campaigns.  
**Prompt Text:**

```text
Context: We are advertising [PRODUCT NAME] on [CHANNEL]. Campaign objective is [CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVE]. The audience is [AUDIENCE]. The offer is [OFFER].
Objective: Write 5 paid social ad variants.
Style: Scroll-stopping but credible.
Tone: [TONE].
Audience: [AUDIENCE].
Response: For each variant, return Primary Text, Headline, Description, CTA and Creative Direction. Do not use fear-based claims or unsupported urgency.
```

**Variables/Placeholders:** `[PRODUCT NAME]`, `[CHANNEL]`, `[CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVE]`, `[AUDIENCE]`, `[OFFER]`, `[TONE]`  
**Example Output:**

**Primary Text:** Close week should not start with five broken spreadsheets. Atlas Analytics gives SaaS finance teams one workflow for recurring revenue reporting and owner follow-up.  
**Headline:** Make revenue reporting easier  
**Description:** Built for SaaS finance workflows.  
**CTA:** Download the checklist  
**Creative Direction:** Show a messy spreadsheet transforming into a clean owner-based reporting board.

**Best Practices/Tips:** Align the offer to funnel stage. Write separate variants for cold and warm audiences. Give creative direction that designers can turn into visuals without guessing.
